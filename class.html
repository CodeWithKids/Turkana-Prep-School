Early Years Foundation Stage

Playgroup

Age: 2 - 3 years

Our Playgroup program focuses on sensory play, early socialization, and basic cognitive development through fun, interactive activities.

  • Structured play activities
  • Language development
  • Motor skills enhancement
  • Social interaction

Pre-Primary 1 (PP1)

Age: 3 - 4 years

PP1 introduces structured learning with a focus on literacy, numeracy, and creative expression in a nurturing environment.

  • Basic numeracy
  • Creative arts
  • Physical development
